Samsung A55

The Samsung Galaxy A55 5G is a mid-range smartphone known for its solid build quality, premium feel, and capable performance. It features a 6.6-inch Super AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, a triple-lens rear camera system, and a long-lasting battery. It also boasts an IP67 rating for dust and water resistance.

Samsung A05

The Samsung Galaxy A05 is a budget-friendly smartphone featuring a large 6.7-inch HD+ display, a dual rear camera system (50MP main + 2MP depth), and an 8MP front-facing camera. It runs on a MediaTek Helio G85 processor with 4GB of RAM and Android 13, with the One UI interface. The device supports dual SIM 4G LTE, dual-band Wi-Fi, and Dolby Atmos audio. It offers 64GB or 128GB of expandable storage (up to 1TB via microSD) and is powered by a 5000mAh battery with 25W fast charging support.

Samsung S25

The Samsung Galaxy S25 series features a sleek design, powerful performance, and advanced AI capabilities. The phones boast Dynamic AMOLED 2X displays with high refresh rates and HDR10+ support. The S25 Ultra includes an S Pen with stylus functionality, while all models utilize Gorilla Glass for display protection and offer IP68 water and dust resistance.
